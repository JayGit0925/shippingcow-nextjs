## Rules
1. **Ask first** — surface assumptions, tradeoffs, ambiguity. Never pick silently.
2. **Minimal code** — no speculative features, no single-use abstractions. 200→50 lines? Rewrite.
3. **Surgical edits** — touch only what's needed, match existing style, clean only your own orphans.
4. **Verify** — every task gets success criteria. Step→check. Loop until green.
